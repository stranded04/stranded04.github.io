"friends"？

They are very cute

Although they are cute, their attack can also make you scared. Being small enough to keep the zombies away from your hut, they are your best guardians in the game, and there are no zombies they can't stop.

They all seem to have no brains, but they all seem to have brains, because their ideas are interesting. They'll do all sorts of things to spite you, and they're actually pretty smart about it.
